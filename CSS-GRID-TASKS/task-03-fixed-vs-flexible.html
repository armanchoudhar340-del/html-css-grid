Answers

1. What happens to Fixed columns when the screen is small?

They stay at 200px no matter what. If the screen is too narrow, they will cause a scrollbar or get cut off because they cannot shrink.

2. Why is 'fr' better for responsive design?

'fr' units allow columns to resize proportionally to fill the available space. They never overflow the container because they are based on percentage logic.

3. Can you mix fixed and flexible units?

Yes! You can use 200px 1fr 1fr. The first column will be fixed, and the other two will share the remaining space.
